Events · AI Coachella Valley · The 760

The agent economy isn't coming.
It's here. We're building
for it in the desert.

AICV events are not AI 101. We run hands-on sessions on the tools that are already reshaping how work gets done — Claude CoWork, Claude Code, and OpenClaw. Autonomous agents. Generative workflows. Digital employees that don't clock out. If you want to understand what that means for your business, your career, or your next venture — this is where you start.

We host workshops, founder meetups, startup sprints, and intelligence briefings for operators, builders, and investors who are paying attention to what's emerging in the Coachella Valley. The valley that hosts Coachella and Stagecoach, that's building Cotino, that sits two hours from Los Angeles and three from San Francisco — is also quietly becoming a serious node in the AI economy. AICV is the intelligence layer making that legible.

If you're visiting, retreating, relocating, or considering where to put early capital — these events are where the conversation starts.

Context

Why These Events Exist

The Coachella Valley's workforce is concentrated in hospitality, healthcare, education, and services. These are the sectors facing the most direct pressure from AI-driven automation — and the ones with the most to gain from getting ahead of it. AICV builds events around one conviction: that the people who live and work here deserve real access to what's coming. Not a demo day. Not a weekend seminar. Real fluency in the tools that are redefining what a productive workforce looks like.

The agent economy is creating a new category of worker — one who manages AI tools, builds generative workflows, and deploys digital employees to handle repeatable cognitive work. We're training that workforce here, in the desert, before the window closes.
