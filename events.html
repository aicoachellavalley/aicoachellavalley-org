Events · AI Coachella Valley · The 760

The agent economy is here.

Hands-on events for founders, operators, and builders in the Coachella Valley.

AI Coachella Valley (AICV) is a fiscally sponsored initiative of Desert Community Foundation focused on preparing the region for an AI-driven economy through workshops, training sessions, and founder events in Palm Desert and across the Coachella Valley.

Since 2025, AICV has hosted 30+ AI workshops and public sessions with 200+ attendees — working with local institutions, small businesses, and professionals to accelerate AI adoption across the region.

Programs

What We Run

AI Workshops

Practical, hands-on training sessions built around real-world AI tools and workflows. No fluff. No slides. You leave with something you can use.

Founder Meetups & Sprints

Conversations, early-stage ecosystem building, and community build sessions for operators and founders paying attention to what's emerging in the valley.

Intelligence Briefings

Regional insights on how AI is reshaping the local economy — workforce shifts, new tools, and what it means for businesses operating in the Coachella Valley right now.

Not AI 101 — working sessions on Claude CoWork, Claude Code, and OpenClaw. Real tools. Real workflows. Real results.

Context

Why These Events Exist

The Coachella Valley's economy is anchored in hospitality, healthcare, education, and service industries — sectors facing both the highest exposure to AI-driven change and the largest upside from early adoption.

The agent economy is creating a new category of worker — one who manages AI tools, builds generative workflows, and deploys digital employees to handle repeatable cognitive work. That includes the next generation of valley workers entering the workforce right now.

We're training that workforce here, in the desert, before the window closes.

Audience

Who Shows Up

Operators & Small Businesses

Business owners across Palm Desert, Palm Springs, Rancho Mirage, La Quinta, Indio, and beyond who want to understand what AI means for their operations — and get ahead of it.

Founders & Builders

Entrepreneurs, developers, and startup teams building in or relocating to the valley. If you're serious about the agent economy, this is your local home base.

Investors & Explorers

VCs, angels, and operators visiting, retreating, or evaluating the valley. If you're considering where to put early capital or where to plant roots — these events are where the conversation starts.
